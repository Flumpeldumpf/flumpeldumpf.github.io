My Work

My most recent work has been with the Universal Jazz Advocates and Mentors Society (UJAM) for the past two years, and with the Victoria Arion Male Choir (VAMC) for the past five years. Work for the VAMC has included recording and mixing the choir, editing youtube videos, writing a composition, and leading sectionals.

Work for UJAM involved more administrative work, including building the UJAM website, managing camp registration, emails, transcribing music for singers for the monthly vocal night, making posters and managing social media, and so much more.
